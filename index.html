Who We Are

Company Overview

OM Smart Solutions is a premier technology and infrastructure solutions provider. We specialize in planning, design, execution, integration, and maintenance of end-to-end systems for municipal administration, enterprise networks, and private developers.

Our core mission centers on creating safer, smarter, and highly sustainable environments. We achieve this by leveraging robust engineering, state-of-the-art AI-driven video analytics, smart grid systems, and structural construction.

Intelligent Traffic Management (ITMS)

Smart Transit & Adaptive Control

Improves urban mobility, road safety, and traffic flow through automation, real-time analytics, and Adaptive Traffic Control Systems (ATCS) which dynamically adjust signal timings based on real-time vehicle flow.

ITMS Core Functionalities

  • Centralized Traffic Control Room
  • Automated Traffic Violation Detection
  • Real-Time Traffic Volume Monitoring
  • Dynamic Congestion Alerts & Routing
  • Neural Network Vehicle Classification
  • National Informatics Centre (NIC) Integration
  • Automated Vehicle Path Tracking

ATCS Features (OM ATCS)

  • Adaptive Signal Timing Optimization
  • Emergency Vehicle Preemption Priority
  • Pedestrian Prioritization Signals
  • Traffic Anomaly & Blockage Detection
  • Reduced Travel Time & Emission Levels
  • Lower Fuel Consumption across corridors
Smart Highway Toll Plazas & Multi-Lane Tolling
